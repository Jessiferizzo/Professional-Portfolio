About Me
Hi, I'm Jessica. Firstly, I have a lot of cats and plants. Secondly, I am a speech therapist by day and a full stack developer by night.
I originally graduated from UT Austin with a psychology/English degree. Researching the why behind people's motivations and then writing about it in short stories, poems, facebook updates, drawings has always been my favorite jam. I then found a love for teaching children with autism and returned to grad school at UT to pursue my Masters in speech pathology.
Today, I seek to leverage all my collected knowledge to design aesthetically pleasing, meaningful apps that leave users feeling refreshed and connected with a faint tinge of joy from having visited my sites.
